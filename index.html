Supersus

In this game you will be given 1 of the 3 factors(spacecrew, impostor, neutral) randomly. Play as a spacecrew where you can achieve victory by completing all tasks or identify and vote out the impostors. Play as a impostor where you have to kill and eliminate everyone and deploy strategic sabotages to create chaos among the crews to win. Play as a neutral where each role comes with different unique objectives to win. In this game, you can comunicate with other people in the match.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:

  • You can use microphone in-match to communicate.
  • The game have a lot of roles and gives role card to increase the chance of getting the role you want.
  • You can switch language to join and play different regions.
  • You can dress up your character.
  • You can play with your friends.
