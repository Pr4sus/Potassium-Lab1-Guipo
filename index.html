5 reasons why this game is my favorite:

Supersus

In this game you will be given 1 of the 3 factors(spacecrew, impostor, neutral) randomly. Play as a spacecrew where you can achieve victory by completing all tasks or identify and cote out the impostors. Play as a impostor where you have to kill and eliminate everyone and deploy strategic sabotages to create chaos among the crews to win. Play as a neutral where each role comes with different unique objectives to win. In this game, you can comunicate with other people in the match.
